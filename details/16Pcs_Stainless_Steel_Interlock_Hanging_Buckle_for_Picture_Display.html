Stainless Steel Picture Hangers for a Secure and Sleek Display

Enhance your wall decor with these 16-piece stainless steel interlock hanging buckles. Their durable construction ensures long-lasting performance, resisting rust and corrosion even in harsh environments.

The unique Z-clip interlocking design eliminates the risk of pictures slipping or falling, offering peace of mind with every installation. The slim 2mm profile ensures a nearly invisible mount that complements your space, allowing your artwork to stand out without any hardware distractions. These brackets are easy to install with simple screw attachment, whether you're mounting on a wall or furniture, giving you a quick and efficient solution for any display.

Perfect for art galleries, home offices, or any setting that requires professional-looking picture hangers, these interlock buckles provide a versatile and reliable option for hanging multiple frames or single pieces. Their strong load-bearing capacity supports heavier canvases and frames, ensuring that your displays remain safe and secure over time.
