Lumiverse 24-Pack Dimmable GU10 LED Bulbs

Transform your home with the soft, warm glow of these GU10 LED bulbs. Designed to enhance any room’s lighting, they provide a comfortable and inviting atmosphere with their 2700K color temperature and 40° beam angle. Whether you're lighting your living room, bathroom, or bedroom, these bulbs deliver even illumination that reduces shadows and minimizes eye strain.

As an energy-efficient replacement for traditional 50W halogen bulbs, these 5.5W LED bulbs consume up to 89% less power and last up to 25,000 hours. Their smooth dimming feature allows you to adjust brightness from 0% to 100%, making it easy to set the perfect mood. UL and Energy Star certified, these bulbs are built to be safe, durable, and high performing, with no harmful UV or IR emissions and free from mercury.

Key Features

  • Dimmable GU10 LED bulbs with 2700K soft warm white color temperature
  • Energy-efficient 5.5W bulbs producing 500lm light (50W equivalent)
  • 40° beam angle for focused and beautiful illumination
  • UL Listed and Energy Star certified for safety and performance
  • Ideal for track lighting, spotlights, and recessed fixtures

Why Choose This Product

These dimmable LED light bulbs are the perfect upgrade for your existing lighting system. They save energy and reduce your electricity bill while providing a long-lasting, reliable light source. The soft white color and wide beam angle make them ideal for both functional and decorative lighting, and the clear glass cover ensures maximum light output. With their ceramic base, these bulbs offer enhanced heat dissipation, keeping them cool and efficient for extended use.
