Magid Comfort Flex Nitrile Safety Gloves

Designed for both comfort and safety, these gloves are a must-have for professionals in high-risk environments. Engineered with 100% nitrile material, they deliver superior chemical resistance and durability, making them ideal for use in labs, workshops, and industrial settings.
