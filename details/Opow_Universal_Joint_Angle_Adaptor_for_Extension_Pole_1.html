Opow Universal Joint Angle Adaptor

The Opow Universal Joint Angle Adaptor is a game-changing tool for handling high or awkward tasks with ease. Whether you're cleaning gutters, dusting ceilings, or replacing light bulbs, this adaptable accessory gives you the flexibility to reach exactly where you need to. With 270 degrees of rotation, it allows smooth angling of your extension pole, helping you complete jobs like window cleaning or painting with greater comfort and efficiency.

Designed for universal compatibility with standard threaded extension poles, this tool is perfect for a variety of applications, including tree pruning, snow removal, and home inspections. Its premium durable composite nylon material ensures long-lasting use and resistance to wear, making it a reliable addition to your toolkit. Note that the adaptor comes without a pole, so you can pair it with your existing equipment for maximum versatility.
