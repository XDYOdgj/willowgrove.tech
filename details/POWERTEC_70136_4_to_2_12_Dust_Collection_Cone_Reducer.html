Power Tec 70136 4" to 2-1/2" Dust Collection Cone Reducer

Enhance your workshop efficiency with the Power Tec 70136 Dust Collection Cone Reducer. This precision-engineered adapter seamlessly connects larger 4" dust hoses to smaller 2-1/2" shop vacuum tubes, allowing for smoother airflow and better dust control. Built with durable ABS plastic, it resists wear and tear, ensuring long-lasting performance even in challenging environments.

Why Choose the Power Tec 70136?

Whether you're using a large dust collection system or need a reliable connection for your portable shop vacuum, this cone reducer delivers unmatched versatility. Its double-sided design ensures a secure and leak-free transition, making it perfect for multi-outlet setups or when connecting high-volume equipment to compact vacuums. Ideal for both dry and wet dust applications, it's a must-have for any serious workshop.
