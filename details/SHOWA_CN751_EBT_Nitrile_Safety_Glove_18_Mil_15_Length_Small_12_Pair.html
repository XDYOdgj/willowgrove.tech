SHOWA CN751 EBT Nitrile Safety Gloves

The SHOWA CN751 EBT Nitrile Safety Gloves offer a modern solution for professionals who need reliable protection and a commitment to the environment. Designed with Eco Best Technology, these gloves are built to resist a wide range of chemicals while being gentle on the planet, as they can break down into natural compounds in an active landfill. This eco-friendly aspect makes them a top choice for those seeking sustainable safety equipment without compromising on performance.

With an 18-mil thick palm and 15-inch length, these gloves provide comprehensive protection for both your hands and wrists during heavy-duty work. The added thickness ensures durability and comfort, while the sleek design allows for easy movement. Featuring wave grip channels, they are ideal for wet tasks, giving you a secure grip on tools and materials to prevent accidents.

These gloves are perfect for use in chemical laboratories, industrial operations, and food handling environments where safety and compliance are critical. The nitrile material offers excellent resistance to oils, acids, and other harmful substances, ensuring your skin remains protected from potential irritation. Plus, they are latex-free, making them a safer and more comfortable alternative to traditional gloves.

Their thin cotton flock lining makes them easy to slip on and off quickly, saving you time and effort throughout the workday. The FDA and EU approvals further validate their food-safe use, making them a versatile option for multiple industries. Whether you're working in a lab, factory, or kitchen, the SHOWA CN751 EBT gloves deliver strength, comfort, and sustainability in every use.
