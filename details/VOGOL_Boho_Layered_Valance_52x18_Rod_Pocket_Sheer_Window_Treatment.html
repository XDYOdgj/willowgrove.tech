VOGOL Boho Layered Valance

Elevate your home's style with this elegant boho valance that blends soft texture and vibrant color in a stunning layered design.

Boho Style with Double Layer Construction

The VOGOL Boho Layered Valance features a double-layered design that combines a warm, linen-textured fabric with a bright, striped print. It offers a rich visual contrast, creating a soft and sophisticated look that adds depth to any window.

Scalloped Wavy Edges for a Unique Touch

The valance is adorned with scalloped wavy edges that bring a whimsical, boho-inspired detail to your home decor. These edges pair beautifully with farmhouse, rustic, or cozy styles, making it a versatile decor accent.

Easy Installation & Compatibility

With a rod pocket design, this valance is simple to hang and works seamlessly with most standard 1.7\" curtain rods. It's designed for convenience without compromising on aesthetics.

Versatile and Low Maintenance

This layered window treatment is perfect for adding a boho vibe to your kitchen, nursery, living room, or bedroom. It's machine washable and easy to clean, making it ideal for busy households who want to keep their decor looking fresh and beautiful.
